John's Fried Oreos

Ingredients

  • Double Stuff Oreos
  • Pancake Batter
  • Canola Oil
  • Vanilla Ice Cream

Step-by-step Instructions:

    1. Get ready to eat like a king
    2. Add water or milk to pancake batter
    3. Toss Oreos into batter
    4. Toss those battered Oreos into cooking Oil
