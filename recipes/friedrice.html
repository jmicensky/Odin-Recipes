John's Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Sesame Seed Oil
  • 2-3 Eggs
  • Frozen Veggies
  • Soy Sauce
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Step-by-step Instructions:

    1. Rinse your rice in water at least two times
    2. Slice up a lot of garlic, don't be shy
    3. Cook the garlic in sesame seed oil at the bottom of the pot for a few minutes
    4. Add the rice to the same pot, let dry rice cook in the oil for a few minutes
    5. Add water, make sure ratio of water to rice is 2:1
    6. Bring to a boil, then turn temp very low and let cook slowly until water is gone
    7. Steam the frozen veggies
    8. Toss steamed veggies in seperate pan with lots of seasoning to char them
    9. Combine cooked rice with veggies in large wok. Adding soy sauce while stirring.
