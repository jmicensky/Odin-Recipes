Fried Chicken

John's Tasty Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken Breasts
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs
  • 2-3 Eggs
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Brown Sugar
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Step-by-step Instructions:

    1. Turn on some very dancey inspiring music
    2. Brine the chicken for 2 hrs
    3. Use garlic press to smash garlic into mushy goodness
    4. Combine all seasonings, and the mushy garlic, with eggs & cream to create batter
    5. Combine the flour, breadcrumbs, and all seasonings to create breading. Put a lot of brown sugar.
    6. Dip the chicken into the breading and batter in this order: Flour - Batter - Flour Again
    7. Place into a hot pot of canola and avocado oil
